Customisable Tremolo


The first audio effect from Muten Audio: a customisable tremolo. Created with flexilbility in mind, this is a plugin which gives you full control of the volume behaviour of each oscillation.
Find your style and shape your sound according to your own taste.
The display allows for instant feedback and clear visualisation of how the plugin will modify your input.

Specification

  • Control the depth of oscillations
  • Set speed and skew the oscillation centre
  • Choose from three oscillation shapes both before and after the centre
  • Full stereo functionality, with the option to have completely independent tremolo effects on the left and right channels
