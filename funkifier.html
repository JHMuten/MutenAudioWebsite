Funk Time


An automatic wah pedal that gives you full control; set the frequency of your 'heel' and 'toe' positions, as well as the attack and decay speeds.
Our custom-built note detection means that the effect will perfectly follow whatever you are playing.
The wah behaviour can be modified via the Q control, and you can get the funk balance just right with the mix control.
